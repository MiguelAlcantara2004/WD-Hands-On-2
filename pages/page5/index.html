Ganyu


Ganyu is a support heroic playable character in the fantasy role-playing video game Genshin Impact. She is a half-human, half-Adeptus hybrid and the secretary to the Liyue Qixing, serving as the final protagonist of the second act of Chapter I, Farewell, Archaic Lord, and one of the tritagonists in the third act, A New Star Approaches.
